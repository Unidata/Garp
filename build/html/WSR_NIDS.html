WSR-88D NIDS Product Descriptions

What is NIDS?

NIDS (Nexrad Information Dissemination System) is the mechanism that allows delivery of a set of standard products from the WSR-88D radars to end users. A set of 4 NIDS vendors receive the radar products and in turn make them available to the general public.

Product Descriptions

  • BREF

    • Base Reflectivity at the 4 lowest tilt angles. Angles depend on radar operation mode: 

    Precipitation Mode    Clear-Air Mode
------------------    --------------
0.50 degrees          0.50 degrees  
1.45                  1.50
2.40                  2.50
3.35                  3.50
    (Individual sites may modify these angles for local needs.)

    Generated in both precipitation and clear-air modes.

  • CREF

    • Composite Reflectivity depicting the highest reflectivity measured vertically above each point in the display.

    Generated in precip and clear-air modes.

  • LREF

    • Layer Composite Reflectivity over 3 altitude depths. 

    1.  Surface to 24,000 ft
2.  24,000  to 33,000 ft
3.  33,000  to 60,000 ft

    Generated in precip mode only.

  • VEL

    • Radial Velocity at the 4 lowest tilt angles. Generated in precip and clear-air modes.

  • TOPS

    • Maximum height of in-cloud echoes. Available in precip mode only.

  • VIL

    • A measure of the total Vertical Integrated Liquid at all points on the image. Precip mode only.

  • PRE1

    • 1-hour precipitation accumulation.

  • PRE3

    • 3-hour precipitation accumulation.

  • PRET

    • Storm total accumulation since the onset of precipitation.

  • Other Non-image Products

    • These are products that we receive that cannot be displayed as an image: 
    VAD   Velocity Azimuth Display
PREA  Hourly digital rainfall array
STAT  General radar status message
