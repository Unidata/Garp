WSI NOWrad Product Descriptions

What is NOWrad?

NOWrad products are value-added image products created by WSI Corporation, using WSR-88D (and other) radar data as input.

NOWrad Products

  • USRAD

    • This is a mosaic image of the lowest level reflectivity over the U.S. at 8-km resolution. It is mapped to a Lambert Conic Conformal projection and has gone through manual quality control prior to transmission.

    A new USRAD image product arrives every 15 minutes.

  • MASTER

    • This is also a reflectivity mosaic over the U.S. but at a higher 2-km resolution. This product is mapped into a Cylindrical Equidistant projection.

    A new MASTER image sector arrives every 5 minutes and does not go through manual quality control.
