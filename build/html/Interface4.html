GARP Speed and Fade Sliders

Speed Slider

By moving the Speed slider, one can adjust the speed of an animation loop. Moving the slider to the left slows it down and moving it to the right speeds up the animation. One can fine tune animation speed with the animation preferences dialog, available either from the "Options" dropdown menu or by clicking the image counter.

Fade Slider

The Fade slider fades out any image data to reveal other graphics that may be difficult to see with multiple overlays.
