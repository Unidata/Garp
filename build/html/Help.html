GARP Help

When you click the GARP Help button on the right side of the main menubar, you see a dropdown menu with several options. Some of the buttons such as About GARP, pop up a simple Motif dialog with information printed in it. Other buttons such as User's Guide launch a browser pointing to a URL (or file). The browser launched by this button must reside on your system and is configurable in "Garp_defaults". Future versions of GARP may include a built-in browser.
