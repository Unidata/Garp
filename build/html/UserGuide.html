GARP User's Guide

(version 2.1)
As you may already know, GARP is an interactive program for displaying meteorological data. We have prepared this GARP User's Guide in hypertext documents to facilitate quick access to the information. GARP is freely available through Unidata to member universities and through the SOO/SAC Program to National Weather Service Offices.
